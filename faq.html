Frequently Asked Questions

What are your office hours?

Our office is open from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. We are closed on weekends and public holidays.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept a wide range of insurance plans, including from major providers like Cigna, Delta Dental, and MetLife. Please call our office with your specific plan details to confirm coverage.

Is there parking available?

Yes, there is free patient parking available in the lot directly behind our building.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require at least 24 hours' notice for any appointment cancellations. A fee may be charged for late cancellations or no-shows.
